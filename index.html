POWERING THE ENERGY TRANSITION

Safe, high-performance solid-state sodium batteries — built for real-world storage.

Elektra Industries develops sustainable solid-state sodium batteries in collaboration with the Advanced Ionics for Sustainable Energy Lab (AISEL) at the University of Calgary. Our patented approach targets a high-energy-density alternative to traditional lithium batteries with lower cost, improved safety, wider operating temperatures, and stronger environmental outcomes.
